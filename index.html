About

I'm Tushar Iyer. I love technology. I've been tinkering with computers since I was a child. I'm currently a 20 year old Computer Science student, but I work as a System Administrator for the University of Hartford's Math, Physics, and Computer Science department. I also work as an International Student Ambassador for the Red Key Society at the University of Hartford.

As a Student Ambassador, I am one of several ambassadors who interact with new incoming international students and help them prepare to move to campus by answering any questions or queries they may have.

I love to travel, and I have lived in multiple cities and countries. I hope to continue travelling post-graduation and would like to visit as many places as I can.
